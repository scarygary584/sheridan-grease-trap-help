Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Sheridan Grease Trap Help handles information associated with calls and website use. The online form on this static website does not store or transmit personal information.

Information you provide

If you call the phone number on this website, you may choose to provide your name, business name, phone number, email address, property address or service area, business type, grease trap or interceptor details, and a description of the service issue. This information may be used to understand the request and seek connection with an available independent provider.

Online form

The online form on this static website does not submit or store personal information. If you press the form button, the page displays a message asking you to call (307) 312-9407 to request service help.

Independent providers

If a request is referred to an independent provider, that provider may have its own privacy practices. Provider qualifications, pricing, response times, service areas, and availability vary.

Website data

Basic technical information may be processed by hosting providers or browser technologies as part of normal website delivery. Do not submit sensitive personal, financial, medical, or emergency information through this website.

Contact

For service-request help, call (307) 312-9407.
